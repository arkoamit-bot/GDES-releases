---

# Mission

The software architecture is now considered stable.

Future development must focus on expanding, validating, and governing medical knowledge rather than adding new software features.

The objective is to transform GDES into a continuously evolving evidence-based nephrology knowledge platform.

The success of this phase will be measured by clinical accuracy, evidence quality, completeness of disease coverage, and expert validation.

---

# Core Philosophy

Software should become increasingly stable.

Medical knowledge should continuously evolve.

Separate software engineering from knowledge engineering.

Architecture should change rarely.

Knowledge should improve continuously.

---

# Strategic Objectives

1. Expand disease coverage.
2. Expand clinical knowledge.
3. Expand treatment knowledge.
4. Expand drug intelligence.
5. Expand monitoring protocols.
6. Expand follow-up recommendations.
7. Build evidence traceability.
8. Build expert-reviewed knowledge.
9. Validate every recommendation.
