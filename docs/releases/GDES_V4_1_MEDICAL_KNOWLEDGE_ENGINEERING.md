# Mission

The software architecture of GDES is now considered mature and stable.

From this point onward, GDES shall be developed primarily as a **Medical Knowledge Engineering Platform**, not as a traditional software project.

Future work should focus on improving the quality, completeness, accuracy, explainability, and validation of clinical knowledge.

Every engineering effort should ultimately improve clinical decision-making.

---

# Philosophy

Software is the infrastructure.

Medical knowledge is the product.

Clinical reasoning is the value.

Patient safety is the objective.

Architecture should remain stable.

Knowledge should continuously evolve.

---

# Primary Goal

Develop GDES into the most comprehensive, evidence-based, explainable, and clinically validated Glomerular Disease Knowledge Platform.

The objective is **clinical completeness**, not software complexity.

---

# Knowledge Engineering Principles

Every recommendation must be:

• Evidence-based

• Guideline-linked

• Clinically explainable

• Version controlled

• Expert reviewed

• Testable

• Auditable

• Continuously updated

No recommendation should exist without supporting evidence.

---

# Disease-Centric Development Strategy

Do not expand the number of diseases.

Instead, deepen each disease until it reaches expert-level completeness.

Each disease becomes an independent knowledge package.

Progress to the next disease only after the current one satisfies predefined quality standards.

# Final Instruction to OpenCode

You are no longer primarily building software.

You are engineering a living, continuously evolving nephrology knowledge system.

Every improvement should increase:

- Clinical accuracy
- Evidence quality
- Explainability
- Consistency
- Patient safety
- Expert trust
- Research value
- Long-term maintainability

When making implementation decisions, prioritize **medical correctness over technical elegance**.

The software architecture should remain stable; the medical knowledge should continuously mature through rigorous evidence synthesis, expert review, structured validation, and comprehensive testing.

The ultimate goal is for GDES to become the definitive digital knowledge platform for glomerular diseases, supporting clinicians, researchers, educators, and patients with transparent, evidence-based, and clinically trustworthy recommendations.
