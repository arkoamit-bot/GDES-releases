### CASE-TG-DSA-002: Treatment-Refractory DSA-Mediated TG
**Type:** atypical
**History:**
48-year-old female, 4 years post-transplant. Despite IVIG + rituximab, DSA MFI rising. Proteinuria 3.8g/g. eGFR declining 50 to 28 over 18 months.
**Examination:**
BP 158/92. Oedema 2+. Weight gain.
**Laboratory Data:**
| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| eGFR (mL/min) | 28 |
| UPCR (g/g) | 3.8 |
| serum albumin (g/dL) | 3.2 |
| DSA DR MFI | 16500 |
| C1q | positive |
**Biopsy Data:**
| Feature | Finding |
|---|---|
| EM | GBM double contours 60% (cg2-cg3) |
| Light microscopy | Glomerulosclerosis 40%, severe IFTA |
| IF | C4d+ PTC |
**Diagnosis:**
Refractory chronic active ABMR with TG cg2-cg3
**Treatment:**
Bortezomib 1.3mg/m2 x 4 cycles. Belatacept conversion. Supportive care.
**Outcome:**
Partial DSA response (MFI 11000). eGFR stabilised at 26 at 6 months.
**Differential Diagnosis:**
| Disease | Score | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| De novo MN | Low | No subepithelial deposits |

**History:**
55-year-old male, 7 years post-transplant. Acute on chronic: sudden rise in proteinuria to 8.2g/g, eGFR drop 40 to 18. CNI everolimus conversion recent. DSA negative.
**Examination:**
BP 172/104. Anasarca. Bruising.

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|

| UPCR (g/g) | 8.2 |
| serum albumin (g/dL) | 2.0 |
| DSA | negative |
| platelets | 110 |
| LDH | 680 |
**Biopsy Data:**
| Feature | Finding |
|---|---|
| EM | GBM double contours + endothelial swelling (TMA features) |
| Light microscopy | Glomerular thrombi, mesangiolysis |
| IF | C4d negative, fibrin+ |

TG with superimposed CNI-related TMA

| Disease | Score | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| De novo TMA | Low | DSA negative, C4d negative |

### CASE-TG-TYP-001: Classic Transplant Glomerulopathy in a 45-Year-Old with DSA
**Type:** typical
**History:**
45-year-old female kidney transplant recipient 6 years post-transplant. eGFR declining from 60 to 35 over 18 months. Proteinuria 2.3g/g. De novo DSA (DQ7) detected at MFI 8500.
**Examination:**
BP 155/95. Oedema 1+ bilateral lower extremities. Stable graft tenderness-free.
**Laboratory Data:**
| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| eGFR (mL/min) | 35 |
| UPCR (g/g) | 2.3 |
| serum albumin (g/dL) | 3.8 |
| DSA DQ7 MFI | 8500 |
| C4d (PTC) | focal positive |
**Biopsy Data:**
| Feature | Finding |
|---|---|
| EM | GBM double contours in 8/15 glomeruli (cg2) |
| Light microscopy | Mesangial expansion, segmental sclerosis |
| IF | C4d+ peritubular capillaries (focal) |
**Diagnosis:**
Transplant Glomerulopathy cg2, chronic active ABMR (DSA+)
**Treatment:**
CNI reduced (tac 5->3). IVIG 2g/kg q4w started. Ramipril 10mg daily. Empagliflozin 10mg daily.
**Outcome:**
Proteinuria stable at 1.8g/g at 6 months. eGFR stable at 33.
**Differential Diagnosis:**
| Disease | Score | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| CNI Nephrotoxicity | Low | No striped fibrosis or arteriolar hyalinosis |
