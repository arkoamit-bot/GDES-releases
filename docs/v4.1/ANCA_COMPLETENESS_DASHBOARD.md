## 1. Overview

This document assesses the completeness of ANCA-associated vasculitis knowledge coverage using the V4.1 12-domain scoring framework. The framework evaluates each domain against defined criteria, assigns a percentage score, and calculates an overall completeness score with a target of greater than or equal to 95%. The assessment covers Disease Knowledge, KB Rules, Guideline Integration, Clinical Pathways, Clinical Cases, Drug Knowledge, Risk Stratification, Evidence Integration, Monitoring Protocols, Complications and Safety, Classification Systems, and Long-Term Outcomes. This baseline assessment is performed at initial knowledge specification publication (v1.0, 2026-07-10).

---

## 2. 12-Domain Scoring Framework

### 2.1 Scoring Methodology

Each domain is scored against its defined criteria on a 0-100% scale. The overall completeness score is the weighted average of all 12 domains. Target: greater than or equal to 95%.

| Score Range | Rating | Action |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| 95-100% | Excellent | Maintain; routine review |
| 85-94% | Good | Address gaps; prioritise updates |
| 70-84% | Adequate | Significant gaps; plan updates |
| Below 70% | Incomplete | Urgent gap closure required |
