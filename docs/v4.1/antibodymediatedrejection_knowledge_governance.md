## 5. Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Non-adherence | Late ABMR, graft loss | MEMS caps, counselling, simplify regimen |
| Subclinical ABMR missed | Progression to chronic ABMR | Protocol biopsy in high-risk patients |
| C4d-negative ABMR underdiagnosed | Missed opportunity for treatment | DSA testing + molecular classifier |
| Refractory ABMR | Rapid graft loss | Bortezomib rescue therapy |
| Recurrent ABMR in re-transplant | Second graft failure | Desensitisation + aggressive monitoring |
