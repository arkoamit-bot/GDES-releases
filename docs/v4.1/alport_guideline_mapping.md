## 5. Gaps and Future Updates

- SGLT2i: not covered in KDIGO 2021; ERA/EDTA 2022 and ISN 2023 provide guidance — expected in KDIGO 2025
- Aortic root screening: no consensus — Alport Syndrome Foundation recommends echo at diagnosis and Q3-5 years
- Gene therapy: no guideline coverage yet — clinical trials ongoing
- No RCTs for any Alport therapy — all evidence from observational studies
