## 5. Gaps and Future Updates

- No RCTs for DDD — all evidence from case series and C3G Registry
- Eculizumab: KDIGO 2025 provides conditional recommendation (2D) for rapidly progressive DDD
- Complement inhibitors (iptacopan, avacopan): phase III trials ongoing — expected KDIGO 2027 update
- Genetic testing: recommended in KDIGO 2025 but cost-effectiveness unclear
- DDD vs C3GN distinction: increasingly recognized as important for treatment and prognosis
