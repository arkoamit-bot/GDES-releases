## 1. Overview

This document maps the major clinical guidelines relevant to ANCA-associated vasculitis with renal involvement, identifies areas of concordance and divergence, and provides cross-reference tables for clinical decision-making. Primary guidelines include KDIGO 2021 Chapter 8, KDIGO 2024 emerging therapies update, EULAR/ERA-EDTA 2016, EULAR 2024 update, ACR/VF 2021, and BSR 2014 guidelines.

## 2. KDIGO 2021 Clinical Practice Guideline for the Management of Glomerular Diseases — Chapter 8: ANCA-Associated Vasculitis

### 2.1 Guideline Overview

**Full title**: KDIGO 2021 Clinical Practice Guideline for the Management of Glomerular Diseases  
**Published**: Kidney International, 2021  
**Volume/Issue**: 100(4S), S1-S276  
**Chapter 8**: ANCA-Associated Vasculitis (pages S161-S198)

### 2.2 Key Recommendations (KDIGO 2021 Ch 8)

| Recommendation | Grade | Evidence Level |
|----------------|-------|---------------|
| Renal biopsy is recommended for diagnosis of AAV GN | 1B | Moderate |
| Rituximab or cyclophosphamide plus glucocorticoids for induction | 1A | High |
| Rituximab preferred in patients who are not good candidates for cyclophosphamide | 1B | Moderate |
| Rituximab 500 mg q6mo x 4 doses for maintenance | 1B | Moderate |
| Azathioprine 1–2 mg/kg/day as alternative maintenance | 1B | Moderate |
| Treat for minimum 18–24 months of maintenance therapy | 1C | Low |
| PLEX not routinely indicated for renal AAV | 2B | Moderate |
| PLEX for severe DAH requiring mechanical ventilation | 2C | Low |
| TMP-SMX PJP prophylaxis during induction | 1A | High |
| Measure ANCA titres at regular intervals | 2C | Low |

### 2.3 KDIGO 2024 Update (Emerging Therapies)

| Recommendation | Grade | Notes |
|----------------|-------|-------|
| Avacopan as adjunctive therapy for induction to reduce steroid exposure | Not graded | Based on ADVOCATE trial |
| Avacopan 30 mg BID for 52 weeks | Not graded | FDA/EMA approved indication |
| Avacopan may be used with rituximab or cyclophosphamide | Not graded | As per ADVOCATE protocol |

### 2.4 KDIGO 2021 — Detailed Mapping

#### Diagnosis and Classification

| KDIGO Recommendation | Evidence | Our Implementation |
|----------------------|----------|-------------------|
| Renal biopsy is gold standard | 1B | Stage 1: Biopsy with Berden classification |
| ANCA testing by immunoassay and IIF | 1B | Stage 1: Combined serology |
| Classify as GPA, MPA, EGPA | 1C | Disease knowledge: CHCC 2012 classification |
| Berden histological classification | 2C | Stage 1: Pathologist assigns class |

#### Induction Therapy

| KDIGO Recommendation | Evidence | Our Implementation |
|----------------------|----------|-------------------|

| Methylprednisolone 1g x 3 pulses if severe | 1B | Stage 2: Pulse protocol |

| PLEX not routine; use only for severe DAH | 2B | Stage 2: PLEX decision branch |
| TMP-SMX prophylaxis mandatory | 1A | Stage 2: PJP prophylaxis |

#### Maintenance Therapy

|----------------------|----------|-------------------|

## 3. EULAR/ERA-EDTA 2016 Recommendations for the Management of AAV

### 3.1 Guideline Overview

**Full title**: European League Against Rheumatism/European Renal Association-European Dialysis and Transplant Association recommendations for the management of ANCA-associated vasculitis  
**Published**: Annals of the Rheumatic Diseases, 2016  
**Volume**: 75(9), pages 1583-1594

### 3.2 Key Recommendations

| Recommendation | LoE | Strength |
|----------------|-----|----------|
| Biopsy confirmation before immunosuppression | 3 | C |
| Rituximab or CyP + steroids for induction (new diagnosis) | 1B+ | A |
| Rituximab preferred for relapsing disease | 1B | B |
| PLEX only in severe RPGN (Cr >5.7) or DAH | 1B | A |
| Maintenance with AZA 2 mg/kg/day or rituximab | 1B | A |
| Minimum 18 months of maintenance | 2 | C |
| Monitor ANCA and B cells | 2 | D |

### 3.3 Comparison with KDIGO 2021

| Topic | EULAR 2016 | KDIGO 2021 | Concordance |
|-------|------------|------------|-------------|
| Induction regimen | Rituximab or CyP | Rituximab or CyP | Concordant |
| Rituximab preference | Relapsing disease | First-line in appropriate patients | Partial (KDIGO broader) |
| PLEX in severe RPGN | Recommended (Cr >5.7) | Not routinely recommended | Discordant |
| Maintenance agent | AZA or rituximab | Rituximab preferred | Partial |
| Maintenance duration | 18 months | 18–24 months | Concordant |

## 4. EULAR 2024 Update Recommendations

### 4.1 New Recommendations

| Recommendation | LoE | Key Change from 2016 |
|----------------|-----|----------------------|
| Avacopan recommended for GPA/MPA to reduce steroid exposure | 1B | New agent integrated |
| Avacopan 30 mg BID for 52 weeks | 1B | First C5aR antagonist |
| PLEX restricted to life-threatening DAH only | 1B | Follows PEXIVAS |
| Steroid-minimising approaches strongly encouraged | 1B | Reduced-dose steroids |
| Rituximab maintenance preferred over AZA for PR3-AAV | 1B | Serotype-specific recommendation |
| Extended maintenance (>24 months) for high relapse risk | 2B | Risk-stratified duration |

### 4.2 Key Changes from 2016

| Change | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| Avacopan added as induction adjunct | ADVOCATE trial |
| PLEX indication narrowed | PEXIVAS trial |
| Steroid dose reduced | PEXIVAS reduced-dose arm |
| Rituximab preferred for PR3 maintenance | MAINRITSAN, RITAZAREM |
| Serotype-specific management | Growing evidence for PR3 vs MPO distinction |
| Risk-stratified duration | Relapse risk drives treatment length |

## 5. ACR/VF 2021 Guideline for the Management of AAV

### 5.1 Guideline Overview

**Full title**: American College of Rheumatology/Vasculitis Foundation Guideline for the Management of Antineutrophil Cytoplasmic Antibody-Associated Vasculitis  
**Published**: Arthritis Care and Research, 2021  
**Volume**: 73(8), pages 1088-1105

### 5.2 Key Recommendations

| Recommendation | Strength | Notes |
|----------------|----------|-------|
| Rituximab preferred over CyP for induction | Conditional | Stronger preference than KDIGO |
| Rituximab preferred over AZA for maintenance | Conditional | Also stronger than KDIGO |
| PLEX only for severe DAH | Conditional | Consistent with PEXIVAS |
| TMP-SMX for PJP prophylaxis | Strong | Consistent |
| Consider avacopan if glucocorticoid-sparing needed | Conditional | ADVOACTE-informed |
| Extended treatment for severe or relapsing disease | Conditional | Risk-stratified approach |

### 5.3 Comparison with KDIGO 2021

| Topic | ACR/VF 2021 | KDIGO 2021 | Concordance |
|-------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| Rituximab vs CyP induction | Rituximab preferred | Either acceptable | Discordant (ACR stronger) |
| Maintenance | Rituximab preferred | Rituximab preferred | Concordant |
| PLEX | Severe DAH only | Severe DAH only | Concordant |
| Steroid protocol | Reduced-dose | Traditional dose | Partial |
| Avacopan | Conditional recommendation | Emerging (2024) | ACR earlier adoption |

## 6. BSR 2014 Guidelines

### 6.1 Guideline Overview

**Full title**: British Society for Rheumatology and British Health Professionals in Rheumatology guideline for the management of ANCA-associated vasculitis  
**Published**: Rheumatology, 2014  
**Volume**: 53(12), pages 2306-2309

### 6.2 Key Recommendations

| Recommendation | Notes |
|----------------|-------|
| Cyclophosphamide + steroids for induction | UK standard at time |
| Rituximab as alternative | Recognised but not first-line |
| PLEX for Cr >500 umol/L or severe DAH | Broader than current |
| AZA for maintenance | Standard |
| TMP-SMX prophylaxis | Mandatory |
| Zoledronic acid for steroid-induced bone loss | Unique recommendation |

### 6.3 Historical Context

The BSR 2014 guidelines pre-date several key trials (RAVE, PEXIVAS, MAINRITSAN, ADVOCATE) and are considered outdated. They are included for reference but KDIGO 2021/2024 and EULAR 2024 should be used for current practice.

## 8. Major Guideline Divergences

| Topic | Divergence | Clinical Implication |
|-------|------------|----------------------|
| Rituximab vs CyP as first-line induction | ACR stronger for rituximab; KDIGO/EULAR give equipoise | U.S. centres prefer rituximab; cost influences elsewhere |
| PLEX for severe renal-only disease | EULAR 2016 recommends (Cr >5.7); KDIGO/PEXIVAS does not | PLEX use declining for renal-only AAV |
| Steroid dosing | PEXIVAS reduced-dose vs traditional | Most centres now use reduced-dose protocols |
| Avacopan timing | All advocate use; no consensus on when to start | Some start day 1; others after stabilisation |
| Maintenance duration | 2 years standard; EULAR 2024 recommends PR3 extension | Risk-stratified duration becoming standard |
