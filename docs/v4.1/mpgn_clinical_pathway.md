## 1. Document Purpose

6-stage clinical pathway for Membranoproliferative GN (MPGN) management.

---

## 2. Pathway Overview

```
STAGE 1: Diagnosis & Classification (30d)
STAGE 2: Treat Underlying Cause (180d)
STAGE 3: IC-MPGN Immunosuppression (360d)
STAGE 4: C3G Supportive/Complement (360d)
STAGE 5: Advanced CKD & Transplant (365d)
STAGE 6: Long-Term Monitoring (730d)
```

## 01. Diagnosis and Classification

**Duration:** 30 days  
**Goal:** Confirm MPGN pattern on LM, classify by IF (IC-MPGN vs C3G), identify underlying cause.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Renal biopsy: confirm MPGN LM pattern (double contours, hypercellularity) | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-DX-01 |
| D2 | IF classification: IgG+C3 = IC-MPGN; C3-dominant = C3G | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-DX-02 |
| D3 | Aetiologic workup: HCV/HBV, SLE serology, SPEP/IFE/sFLC, complement, C3NeF, cryoglobulins | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-DX-06 |
| D4 | Complement profiling: C3, C4, CH50, C3NeF | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-DX-04 |
| D5 | EM: subendothelial deposits, define deposit characteristics | ISN 2023 | MPGN-DX-03 |
| D6 | Assess severity: Cr, eGFR, UPCR, BP, crescents | ERA/EDTA 2022 | MPGN-PR-02 |

## 02. Treat Underlying Cause

**Duration:** 180 days  
**Goal:** First-line: treat identified underlying cause. DAAs for HCV, antibiotics for infection, immunosuppression for SLE, clone-directed therapy for MGUS.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| U1 | HCV: DAAs (glecaprevir/pibrentasvir or sofosbuvir/velpatasvir 12 wks) | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-01 |
| U2 | HBV: entecavir/tenofovir + consider immunosuppression cautiously | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-01 |
| U3 | SLE: steroids + MMF or CYC per lupus nephritis protocol | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-01 |
| U4 | Infection: treat source (antibiotics for endocarditis, shunt removal) | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-01 |
| U5 | MGUS: clone-directed therapy (bortezomib/dexamethasone, daratumumab) | ISN 2023 | MPGN-TX-01 |
| U6 | Monitor: monthly Cr, UPCR, C3/C4 during treatment | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-01 |

## 03. IC-MPGN: Immunosuppression

**Duration:** 360 days  
**Goal:** For idiopathic IC-MPGN: corticosteroids + MMF or CYC. Rituximab for refractory.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| I1 | Prednisone 1 mg/kg/day (max 80 mg) x8 weeks then taper over 6-12 mo | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-02 |
| I2 | MMF 1-2 g/day PO divided BID or IV CYC 500-750 mg/m2 monthly x3-6 | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-03 |
| I3 | PCP prophylaxis (TMP/SMX) during immunosuppression | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-01 |
| I4 | Assess response at 3 and 6 months: UPCR, eGFR, C3 | ERA/EDTA 2022 | MPGN-PR-01 |
| I5 | If refractory: rituximab 375 mg/m2 weekly x4 | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-04 |

## 04. C3G: Supportive Care and Complement Inhibition

**Duration:** 360 days  
**Goal:** C3G management: supportive care first-line. Complement inhibitors for progressive disease.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Supportive: RAASi, BP target <130/80, statin, diuretics | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-07 |
| C2 | Monitor C3, C3NeF, CH50 trends; falling C3 precedes flare | ISN 2023 | MPGN-MON-03 |
| C3 | Eculizumab 900 mg IV weekly x4 then 1200 mg q2w if progressive | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-05 |
| C4 | Meningococcal vaccination (required before eculizumab) | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-03 |
| C5 | PLEX if CFH autoantibody positive | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-06 |
| C6 | Consider clinical trial: iptacopan, vemircopan for refractory | KDIGO 2025 | MPGN-TX-08 |

## 05. Advanced CKD and Transplant Planning

**Duration:** 365 days  
**Goal:** Manage advanced CKD (G4-G5) and plan for RRT with consideration of high recurrence risk.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | CKD management: RAASi, anemia, CKD-MBD, metabolic acidosis | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-02 |
| A2 | Discuss high recurrence risk: C3G 50-80%, IC-MPGN 30-50% | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-PR-04 |
| A3 | C3G: consider pre-emptive eculizumab peri-transplant | ISN 2023 | MPGN-MON-04 |
| A4 | Living donor transplant acceptable with informed consent | ERA/EDTA 2022 | MPGN-REF-01 |
| A5 | Protocol biopsy at 3 and 12 months post-transplant | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-04 |

## 06. Long-Term Monitoring

**Duration:** 730 days  
**Goal:** Sustained monitoring for relapse, transplant recurrence, and complications.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Q3 months: eGFR, UPCR, C3, C3NeF (C3G), BP | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-02 |
| L2 | Q6 months: albumin, lipids, Hb, HCO3 | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-MON-02 |
| L3 | C3G: complement trend monitoring — falling C3 predicts relapse | ISN 2023 | MPGN-MON-03 |
| L4 | Lifelong BP, proteinuria, CV risk management | KDIGO 2021 | MPGN-TX-07 |
| L5 | Patient education: infection warning signs, medication adherence | ERA/EDTA 2022 | MPGN-REF-01 |

| Transplant recurrence counselling | 100% |
