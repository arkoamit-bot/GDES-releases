# Alport Syndrome — Clinical Pathway Specification

**Document ID:** ALP-CP-v1.0
**Date:** 2026-07-10
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Final
**Domain:** Clinical Pathways

---

## 1. Document Purpose

6-stage clinical pathway for Alport Syndrome management.

---

## 2. Pathway Overview

```
STAGE 1: Diagnosis and Genetic Confirmation (90d) -> STAGE 2: Renoprotective Therapy Initiation (180d) -> STAGE 3: Hearing and Vision Surveillance (365d) -> STAGE 4: Genetic Counseling and Family Planning (180d) -> STAGE 5: ESKD Preparation and Transplant (365d) -> STAGE 6: Long-Term Multidisciplinary Follow-Up (730d)
```

## 01. Diagnosis and Genetic Confirmation

**Duration:** 90 days
**Goal:** Confirm Alport syndrome: genetic testing, renal biopsy, audiometry, ophthalmology.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Genetic testing: COL4A5/COL4A3/COL4A4 panel — confirm diagnosis and inheritance pattern | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-DX-03 |
| D2 | Renal biopsy: EM for GBM lamellation; IF for alpha-5(IV) staining | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-DX-02 |
| D3 | Skin biopsy: alpha-5(IV) immunofluorescence (diagnostic for XLAS males) | ISN 2023 | ALP-DX-07 |
| D4 | Audiometry: bilateral high-frequency sensorineural hearing loss | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-DX-05 |
| D5 | Ophthalmology: slit lamp for lenticonus, fundoscopy for retinopathy | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-DX-06 |
| D6 | Family history: pedigree construction, cascade testing of at-risk relatives | ISN 2023 | ALP-PR-01 |

**Next stages:** ALP-PATH-02
**Criteria to proceed:** Diagnosis confirmed; proceed to treatment and monitoring

## 02. Renoprotective Therapy Initiation

**Duration:** 180 days
**Goal:** Start RAASi + SGLT2i as soon as proteinuria detected. BP management.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| T1 | Start ACEi/ARB: titrate to maximum tolerated dose (proteinuria reduction 40-60%) | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-TX-01 |
| T2 | Add SGLT2i: dapagliflozin 10 mg if eGFR >=20 and proteinuria >=0.5 g/day | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-TX-02 |
| T3 | BP management: target <130/80; add CCB/thiazide if needed | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-TX-03 |
| T4 | Monitor K+ and Cr 2-4 wks after initiation/dose change | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-MON-01 |

**Next stages:** ALP-PATH-03, ALP-PATH-06
**Criteria to proceed:** Therapy established; proceed to surveillance

## 03. Hearing and Vision Surveillance

**Duration:** 365 days
**Goal:** Annual hearing and vision monitoring. Hearing aids when needed.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | Annual audiometry: monitor high-frequency hearing decline | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-MON-02 |
| H2 | Hearing aid fitting when hearing loss symptomatic | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-TX-06 |
| H3 | Annual ophthalmology: slit lamp for lenticonus progression | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-MON-02 |
| H4 | Speech therapy if childhood hearing loss affects language development | ISN 2023 | ALP-TX-06 |

**Next stages:** ALP-PATH-04, ALP-PATH-06
**Criteria to proceed:** Extra-renal surveillance ongoing

## 04. Genetic Counseling and Family Planning

**Duration:** 180 days
**Goal:** Comprehensive genetic counseling for patients and families.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| G1 | Genetic counseling: discuss inheritance pattern, recurrence risk | ISN 2023 | ALP-TX-08 |
| G2 | XLAS male: all daughters are carriers, no sons affected | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-PR-01 |
| G3 | ARAS: 25% recurrence risk; carrier testing for partner recommended | ISN 2023 | ALP-TX-08 |
| G4 | Preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD) available for severe mutations | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-GN-01 |
| G5 | Cascade screening of at-risk relatives | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-DX-03 |

**Next stages:** ALP-PATH-02, ALP-PATH-06
**Criteria to proceed:** Counseling complete; continue management

## 05. ESKD Preparation and Transplant

**Duration:** 365 days
**Goal:** Prepare for RRT when eGFR <20. Living donor transplant preferred.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | RRT planning: eGFR <20, discuss dialysis and transplant | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-TX-04 |
| E2 | Living donor evaluation: COL4A5+ females may donate (low risk) | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-TX-04 |
| E3 | Post-transplant anti-GBM monitoring: hematuria, Cr, antibodies | ISN 2023 | ALP-TX-05 |
| E4 | No Alport recurrence in transplant — reassure patient | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-PR-04 |
| E5 | Aortic root echo pre-transplant | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-REF-01 |

**Next stages:** ALP-PATH-06
**Criteria to proceed:** Transplant or dialysis initiated

## 06. Long-Term Multidisciplinary Follow-Up

**Duration:** 730 days
**Goal:** Lifelong multidisciplinary follow-up: nephrology, audiology, ophthalmology, cardiology.

### Actions

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Q3 months: eGFR, UPCR, BP, K+ | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-MON-01 |
| L2 | Annual: audiometry, ophthalmology | ERA/EDTA 2022 | ALP-MON-02 |
| L3 | Echo every 3-5 years: aortic root diameter | ISN 2023 | ALP-MON-02 |
| L4 | Post-transplant: anti-GBM surveillance for 5 years | KDIGO 2021 | ALP-MON-03 |
| L5 | Patient support: Alport Syndrome Foundation resources | ISN 2023 | ALP-REF-01 |

**Next stages:** None (final stage)
**Criteria to proceed:** N/A (final stage)
