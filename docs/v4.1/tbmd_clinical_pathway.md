# Thin Basement Membrane Nephropathy (TBMD) — Clinical Pathway

**ID:** TBMD-CP-v1.0
**Date:** 2026-07-10

---

## Stage 1: Diagnosis and Confirmation (30d)

**Goal:** Confirm TBMD: persistent glomerular hematuria + GBM thinning on EM + normal function + no extra-renal signs.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | History: hematuria timing, family history of hematuria/ESKD, hearing/vision | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-DX-01 |
| D2 | Urinalysis: dysmorphic RBCs, RBC casts, quantify proteinuria | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-DX-03 |
| D3 | eGFR: confirm normal for age; BP check | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-DX-03 |
| D4 | Renal biopsy: EM for GBM thickness measurement | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-DX-02 |
| D5 | Audiometry and ophthalmology: exclude Alport syndrome | ERA/EDTA 2022 | TBMD-DX-05 |
| D6 | Diagnosis communication: REASSURE patient of benign nature | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-01 |

## Stage 2: Baseline Assessment and Patient Education (90d)

**Goal:** Complete baseline assessment. Provide comprehensive patient education about TBMD's benign nature.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| B1 | Baseline labs: eGFR, UPCR, BP, urine dipstick | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-MON-01 |
| B2 | Offer genetic testing: COL4A3/COL4A4 for confirmation and carrier status | ISN 2023 | TBMD-DX-07 |
| B3 | Patient education: TBMD is BENIGN — no treatment, normal life, normal lifestyle | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-01 |
| B4 | Avoid nephrotoxins counseling: NSAIDs, aminoglycosides | ERA/EDTA 2022 | TBMD-TX-03 |
| B5 | Discuss family implications: 50% transmission, Alport carrier | ISN 2023 | TBMD-TX-04 |

## Stage 3: Annual Monitoring (365d)

**Goal:** Annual surveillance for proteinuria development, hypertension, eGFR decline.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| M1 | Annual: BP, eGFR, urine dipstick | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-MON-01 |
| M2 | If proteinuria detected: UPCR, serum albumin | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-MON-02 |
| M3 | If stable for 2-3 years: discharge to primary care with advice | ERA/EDTA 2022 | TBMD-MON-01 |
| M4 | Pregnancy: monitor each trimester | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-06 |

## Stage 4: Genetic Counseling and Family Planning (180d)

**Goal:** Comprehensive genetic counseling for patients of reproductive age with confirmed COL4 mutation.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| G1 | Genetic counseling: explain autosomal dominant inheritance, 50% transmission | ISN 2023 | TBMD-REF-01 |
| G2 | Discuss implications: homozygous = Alport syndrome, heterozygous = TBMD carrier | ISN 2023 | TBMD-GN-01 |
| G3 | Offer cascade testing of first-degree relatives | ERA/EDTA 2022 | TBMD-REF-01 |
| G4 | Insurance counseling: TBMD is benign, should not affect coverage | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-05 |

## Stage 5: Re-Evaluation for Alport Syndrome (180d)

**Goal:** If proteinuria >1 g/day or eGFR declining or hearing loss develops — re-evaluate for Alport syndrome.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | Repeat audiometry and ophthalmology | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-PR-01 |
| A2 | Genetic testing: COL4A5, COL4A3, COL4A4 full sequencing | ISN 2023 | TBMD-EX-01 |
| A3 | Consider re-biopsy if proteinuria or eGFR decline unexplained | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-MON-04 |
| A4 | If Alport confirmed: treat per Alport syndrome protocol | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-PR-02 |
| A5 | RAASi for proteinuria management | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-02 |

## Stage 6: Long-Term Outcomes and Discharge (730d)

**Goal:** Confirm stable course. Discharge to primary care with clear instructions if stable.

| ID | Action | Evidence | KB Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Annual monitoring for 2-3 years to confirm stability | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-MON-01 |
| L2 | If stable: discharge to primary care with instructions for annual dipstick | ERA/EDTA 2022 | TBMD-MON-01 |
| L3 | Provide patient letter: diagnosis, benign prognosis, no treatment needed | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-01 |
| L4 | Advise: return if macroscopic hematuria, new edema, or family member diagnosed with Alport | ISN 2023 | TBMD-TX-05 |
| L5 | Document for insurance/employment: TBMD is benign, no restrictions | KDIGO 2021 | TBMD-TX-05 |
