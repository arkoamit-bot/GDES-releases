## 3. Key Drugs

### 3.1 ACE Inhibitors
- Ramipril 2.5-10 mg daily. Titrate to max tolerated.
- Monitor K+, Cr at 2-4 wks.
- Indicated for all with proteinuria >=0.5 g/day.

### 3.2 Rituximab (MGRS-FGN)
- 375 mg/m2 IV weekly x4
- Screen HBV before use
- Monitor CD20+ B cells
- Premedicate with acetaminophen, diphenhydramine

### 3.3 Daratumumab (Plasma Cell Clone)
- 16 mg/kg IV weekly x8 then q4w
- Pre-infusion: corticosteroids, antihistamines, antipyretics
- Monitor for infusion reactions
- HBV reactivation risk

### 3.4 Corticosteroids (Crescentic FGN)
- Prednisone 1 mg/kg/day x8 wks then taper
- Pulse MP 500-1000 mg IV x3 for RPGN

### 3.5 MMF
- 1-2 g/day PO divided BID
- Monitor CBC, LFT monthly

## 4. Drug Safety

| Drug | Key Side Effects | Monitoring |
|---|---|---|
| ACEi | Cough, hyperkalemia, AKI | K+, Cr |
| Rituximab | Infusion reaction, PML, HBV reactivation | HBV screen, CBC |
| Daratumumab | Infusion reaction, cytopenias | CBC, LFT |
| Prednisone | Hyperglycemia, osteoporosis, infection | Glucose, bone density |
| MMF | Myelosuppression, GI, teratogenic | CBC, LFT |
| Cyclophosphamide | Myelosuppression, cystitis, infertility | CBC, UA |
