## 1. Document Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive cross-reference mapping of clinical practice guidelines relevant to Cryoglobulinemic GN management. It aligns KDIGO 2021 Chapter 10, KDIGO 2025, EASL 2020, ISN 2023, ERA/EDTA 2022, ACR 2021, and AASLD/IDSA 2018 recommendations with the BGDDR v4.1 CryoGN knowledge schema.
