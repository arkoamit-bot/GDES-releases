## 1. Document Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive cross-reference mapping of all major clinical practice guidelines relevant to C3 Glomerulopathy management. It aligns KDIGO 2021 (Chapter 8), KDIGO 2025 (Emerging), ERA/EDTA 2022, ISN 2023, Complement Consensus 2023, and Rare Kidney Disease 2019 recommendations with the BGDDR v4.1 disease knowledge schema, clinical pathways, and knowledge base rules. The mapping identifies areas of concordance, divergence, and gaps across guidelines.

## 13. Knowledge Gaps and Future Directions

- No high-grade evidence (1A or 1B) for any C3G treatment
- Optimal timing of complement inhibitor initiation not established
- Paediatric-specific treatment guidelines absent
- Cost-effectiveness of complement inhibitors vs MMF+steroids
- Biomarkers for predicting treatment response (beyond C3 normalisation)
- Long-term safety data for iptacopan and pegcetacoplan
- Role of dual complement pathway blockade (C5 + C3)
