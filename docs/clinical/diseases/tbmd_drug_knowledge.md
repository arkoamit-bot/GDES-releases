# Thin Basement Membrane Nephropathy (TBMD) — Drug Knowledge

**ID:** TBMD-DRUG-v1.0
**Date:** 2026-07-10

---

## Key Point

**No drug treatment is required for isolated hematuria.** This is the most important management message.

## Drugs Used (if proteinuria develops)

| Drug | Dose | Indication |
|---|---|---|
| ACEi (lisinopril, ramipril) | Standard doses | Proteinuria >0.5 g/day |
| ARB (losartan, valsartan) | Standard doses | ACEi intolerant |

## Drugs to AVOID

- NSAIDs (avoid chronic use)
- Aminoglycosides (avoid where possible)
- Any nephrotoxin

## Drug Selection

```
TBMD with isolated hematuria: NO DRUGS
    |
Proteinuria >0.5 g/day: ACEi/ARB
    |
Proteinuria >1 g/day: Re-evaluate for Alport syndrome
```
