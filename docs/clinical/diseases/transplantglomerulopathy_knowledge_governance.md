## 5. Risk Register
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Irreversible CG lesions at diagnosis | Graft loss | Early protocol biopsy + DSA monitoring |
| Misdiagnosis as TCMR | Inappropriate steroids | C4d, DSA, EM for CG confirmation |
| Nephrotic syndrome | Morbidity, infections | ACEi/ARB + SGLT2i + diuretics |
| Infection from intensified IS | Sepsis, PTLD | Screening, prophylaxis, EBV monitoring |
| Rapid graft loss in non-adherence | Early dialysis | Psychosocial support + adherence programme |
