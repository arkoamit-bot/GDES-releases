## 1. Document Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive cross-reference mapping of clinical practice guidelines relevant to Infection-Related Glomerulonephritis management. It aligns KDIGO 2021 Chapter 9, KDIGO 2025, ISN 2023, ERA/EDTA 2022, EULAR 2022, and IDSA 2021 recommendations with the BGDDR v4.1 IRGN knowledge schema.

## 5. Key Knowledge Gaps

- No role for antibiotics in preventing PSGN once established
- Optimal duration of immunosuppression in crescentic disease
- Therapeutic trials for non-streptococcal IRGN
- Long-term cardiovascular outcomes data
